Five Most famous Places You Need to Visit in Sri Lanka
Sigiriya Rock Fortress
Explore this UNESCO World Heritage site, an ancient rock fortress with spectacular frescoes, water gardens, and panoramic views from the top of the 200-meter high column of rock.
Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic
Visit the revered Buddhist temple in Kandy that houses the relic of the tooth of Buddha, surrounded by beautiful architecture and serene lake views in Sri Lanka's cultural capital.
Galle Fort
Wander through this historic Dutch colonial fortress with its charming streets, boutique shops, and sea walls offering stunning views of the Indian Ocean.
Yala National Park
Experience incredible wildlife including the highest density of leopards in the world, along with elephants, sloth bears, and hundreds of bird species in this diverse ecosystem.
Ella
Discover this picturesque hill country town famous for breathtaking mountain views, lush tea plantations, and the iconic Nine Arch Bridge railway viaduct.
